DDM

Digital Experience Museum

Enriched with advanced technological features such as artificial intelligence, virtual and augmented reality, touch screens, infrared technology, and depth perception sensors, the exhibitions promise visitors an environment where they can not only see but also interact. Creativo Milano developed the software behind technologies delivering three- and four-dimensional experiences, with the museum offering both virtual and augmented reality journeys throughout the visit.
