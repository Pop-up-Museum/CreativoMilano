Service — Monuments

Structures built to outlast the moment they mark.

We design and build monuments and landmarks that carry civic and cultural meaning — from concept sculpture to the engineering that lets them stand for generations.

A monument has to work at every scale — as a symbol seen from across a city, and as a detail examined up close. Our process moves from concept sculpture and symbolism through structural engineering and material selection, so the final piece is as sound as it is meaningful.

What we design and build

Concept & symbolic design

Translating a historic or cultural narrative into a form that reads clearly at monumental scale.

Structural engineering

Steel, marble and composite structures engineered to hold complex sculptural forms safely outdoors.

Material & finish selection

Marble, metal cladding and lighting chosen for both visual impact and long-term weather resistance.

Lighting & night presence

Illumination design that gives a monument a second, equally striking identity after dark.

Public space integration

Designing the plaza, approach and surrounding landscape as part of the monument's experience.

Turnkey construction

On-site construction management from foundation to final inauguration ceremony.
