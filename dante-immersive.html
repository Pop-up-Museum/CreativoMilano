Dante's Inferno,
made to walk through.

A full-scale immersive experience bringing the Divine Comedy to life through projection, sound, and virtual reality — designed and built by Creativo Milano in the heart of Florence.

"Nel mezzo del cammin di nostra vita, mi ritrovai per una selva oscura..."

Dante Alighieri, Inferno — Canto I
01 — Dark Forest

Lost, before being found.

Guests step into the tangled dark wood where Dante's journey begins — disoriented, surrounded by shadow, before Virgil appears to guide the way forward.

Selva Oscura
02 — Mirror Room

Reflections of the underworld.

A mirrored chamber multiplies Charon's crossing into infinity — the boundary between the living world and the Inferno, rendered as an endless, disorienting threshold.

Sala degli Specchi
03 — Circles of Hell

Nine circles, one descent.

From Limbo to the river of blood, each circle of the Inferno is staged as its own environment — guiding visitors deeper through Dante's vision of sin and consequence.

Nove Cerchi
04 — Immersive Room

Where the room becomes the story.

Floor-to-ceiling projection turns a historic Florentine hall into the gates of the giants — scale and sound designed to make the audience feel physically small before the myth.

Sala Immersiva
05 — VR Room

The final descent, in first person.

The experience closes with a personal VR sequence — after walking through the Inferno as an observer, each visitor faces its final circle alone, headset on, fully inside the vision.
