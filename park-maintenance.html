Service — Ongoing care

A park never stops working. Neither do we.

Rides, water systems, façades and show elements all age differently — Creativo Milano keeps every part of a park running safely, looking sharp, and ready for its next season.

Designing and building a park is only half the story. Once the gates open, daily wear, weather, and constant use start testing every ride, surface and system. Our maintenance service extends the same care from construction into the everyday life of a park — so what we build keeps delivering the experience it was designed for, year after year.

What ongoing maintenance covers

Technical & mechanical maintenance

Scheduled servicing of rides, motors, hydraulics and control systems to keep every attraction operating within spec.

Safety & compliance inspections

Regular structural and safety checks aligned with international standards, with full documentation for operators and authorities.

Seasonal opening & closing

Pre-season commissioning and end-of-season winterization, so every system is ready the moment doors open again.

Refurbishment & repainting

Refreshing themed surfaces, props and façades so the park keeps the same visual impact it had on opening day.

Spare parts & technical support

Direct access to original components and technical documentation, minimizing downtime when something needs replacing.

24/7 emergency assistance

A direct line to our technical team for urgent issues, so a park is never left waiting during operating hours.
