The "Start a project" form

If you use the "Start a project" chat on this site, the answers you give — including your name and email address — are sent directly to our team via a third-party email delivery service (EmailJS) and land in our inbox at info@creativomilano.it. We use this information only to respond to your inquiry. We don't sell it, and we don't use it for marketing unless you separately opt in elsewhere.

EmailJS processes this data solely to deliver the message and does not use it for any other purpose. You can find their own privacy practices at emailjs.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Your rights

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you can ask us to:

  • Tell you what personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Stop processing your data for a specific purpose

To exercise any of these, email info@creativomilano.it. You can also lodge a complaint with the Italian data protection authority (Garante per la Protezione dei Dati Personali) if you believe your rights haven't been respected.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page as the site evolves — for example if we add new tools or forms. The "last updated" date at the top will always reflect the latest version.

This page is written in plain language to be genuinely useful to visitors, and covers the cookies and data flows actually used on this site today. It isn't a substitute for formal legal advice — before relying on it as your final, published policy, it's worth a quick review by a lawyer familiar with GDPR and Italian data protection law.
