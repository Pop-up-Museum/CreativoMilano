Service — Museums

Museums people want to walk through twice.

We design and build interactive, digital experience museums — spaces where projection, sound and storytelling turn history and ideas into something visitors feel, not just read.

A museum today competes with every screen in a visitor's pocket. Our approach treats exhibition design as experience design: content planning, spatial narrative, projection and interactive technology all developed together from day one, so the building itself becomes part of the story being told.

What we design and build

Immersive projection rooms

Floor-to-ceiling and multi-wall projection environments that turn entire rooms into the exhibit.

Interactive & sensory installations

Touch, motion and sound-responsive exhibits that invite visitors to trigger the story themselves.

Exhibition & content design

From curatorial narrative to wall panels and wayfinding — content planned to work in any language and layout.

Virtual & augmented reality

VR sequences and AR layers that extend the physical exhibit into first-person digital experiences.

Technical fabrication

In-house production of display structures, props and finishes, built to museum durability standards.

Turnkey installation

Full delivery on site — AV integration, calibration and commissioning before opening day.
