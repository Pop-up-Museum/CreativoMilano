Independence Monument

Tashkent, Uzbekistan

City & country
Tashkent, Uzbekistan
Year
2022
Type
National monument
Setting
Star-shaped public plaza

Rising from a star-shaped plaza in the heart of Tashkent, the Independence Monument is a landmark tribute to Uzbekistan's sovereignty. Fountains and national flags frame its sweeping, sail-like silhouette, crowned by a golden bird in flight above an illuminated globe. At its base, bronze sculptures recall the caravan routes of the Silk Road, while carved reliefs anchor the site in the nation's history. Integrated lighting and choreographed fireworks give the monument an equally striking presence after dark.

A distinctive detail — the monument has become the backdrop for Uzbekistan's national Independence Day celebrations, drawing large crowds for its annual firework displays.
